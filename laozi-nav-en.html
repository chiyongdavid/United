Laozi Navigation System

Decision Dashboard – Five Dimensions Check
What this tool does: This is a decision calibration system. It helps you see clearly whether the direction is correct (Dao), whether you can sustain it (De), and if the timing is right.
1. Dao (Alignment)
Is this decision consistent with the underlying pattern of reality?
2. De (Sustainability)
Can I (or the system) carry this through without overstraining?
3. Conditions (External Factors)
Are the necessary resources and objective factors in place?
4. Means (Capabilities)
Do we have the tools and methods required to execute?
5. Timing (The Moment)
Is this the right moment? Not too early, not too late?
